Community Guides

Practical tips for a clean, safe, and healthy Sebayeng

Keep Your Community Clean

  • Dispose of rubbish in designated bins or at collection points
  • Separate recyclables (plastic, glass, paper) from general waste
  • Participate in monthly community clean-up days (every 1st Saturday)
  • Report illegal dumping to municipality at (015) 590 2000 (tap to call)
  • Keep yards clear of stagnant water to prevent mosquitoes
  • Start a street committee to monitor cleanliness in your area
Next clean-up: First Saturday of each month, meet at Tiangmaatla Centre at 8AM

Keep Your Community Safe

  • Form or join a neighbourhood watch group in your area
  • Always lock doors and windows, even when at home
  • Install motion-sensor lights around your property
  • Know your neighbours and look out for suspicious activity
  • Keep emergency numbers saved on your phone (tap any number to dial)
  • Report any crime to Sebayeng SAPS immediately: 10111
Emergency: In any danger, call 10111 immediately (tap to dial)

Keep Your Community Healthy

  • Wash hands regularly with soap, especially before eating
  • Drink clean, boiled or treated water - boil for at least 3 minutes
  • Visit your local clinic for regular check-ups and vaccinations
  • Know your HIV status - free testing at Sebayeng Clinic
  • Attend health workshops at Tiangmaatla Centre
  • Speak to a social worker if you need mental health support: (015) 590 2376
Sebayeng Clinic: Mon-Fri 7:30AM-4PM | Sat 8AM-12PM
